Music SDCS

In the music area of the Diocesano Seminario of Christ the Priest. Young people have a space to explore music and art. From a young age, the love of music is instilled in them. Teaching them about musical notes, instruments and the different types there are, how to make symphonies, read sheet music and how to play instruments. At the same time, at the flag raising, the children can have a space to demonstrate their talent, whether by singing, reading a poem or playing an instrument.

What will your child learn?

If you are interested in your children learning about the art of music, developing skills in creating podcasts, and acquiring a wide variety of knowledge, this school is the right one for them. Our educational program is designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and a passion for learning across multiple disciplines.

Podcasts

In the field of media, the music area has implemented a pedagogy on the development of podcasts. Students will learn how to conduct interviews, record and edit audio.

Support ideas and be able to speak in public

If you are looking for an education that prepares your children to support ideas and speak in public with confidence and clarity, the music area implements this option. With a comprehensive focus on developing argumentation and public speaking skills, students are offered an experience to become effective communicators and leaders, so that your children can reach their full potential.

The art of music

The music program offers students the opportunity to explore and develop their musical talent. With classes ranging from music theory and composition to performance and production, children have the opportunity to learn to play various instruments and participate in our Taloha competition.

She argues that the music area is one of the most dynamic in the school, that the classes are very good, and on the other hand she says that Professor Pablo Piza is a very good teacher, with good teaching methods.
