Taloha

Taloha where "talent is what there is" was the inspiring initiative launched by Professor Pablo Andrés Piza Valendia, with the aim of offering a platform where students could show and develop their various artistic skills. This project not only aimed to reveal hidden talents among young people, but also to foster self-confidence and appreciation for the arts.

A Chance to Shine with Taloha

Professor Piza, known for his dedication to education and his passion for art, believed strongly in the creative potential of every student. Through “Taloha”, he gave them the opportunity to express themselves and shine in a variety of disciplines. The students participated enthusiastically in different categories. In the show, there are juries who will rate the performances and paintings. While the students taste the food. In addition, at the end the winners of the talent show receive a bonus in the store and recognition.

Kitchen

Aspiring chefs prepared impressive dishes, demonstrating their creativity and mastery of culinary techniques. These events became authentic gastronomic festivals where innovative flavors and presentations were appreciated.

Music

Musicians playing a wide range of instruments, such as piano, guitar, violin and drums, gave recitals that showcased their technical skill and passion for music. Additionally, there are vocal performances that include a diversity of genres.

Art

Visual artists displayed their works in exhibitions that allowed the educational community to appreciate their talent and artistic vision. Students explored varied techniques, from painting and drawing to sculpture.
