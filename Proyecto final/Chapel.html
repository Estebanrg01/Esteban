Chapel

In the chapel, Professor Pablo Andrés Piza helps with liturgical songs and the participation of students in the Eucharists. His work began in 2012 and lasted until 2015, a period during which he dedicated himself exclusively to liturgical songs.

The music area: A Pillar in the Liturgy and Religious Education of the Seminary

In 2017, Pablo Andrés Piza returned to the seminary to be part of the liturgy, in addition to serving as a music teacher and professor in the area of religion. The main objective of the chapel is to promote the faith and spiritual construction of the students, to achieve a deeper approach to the Christian faith.

Important events

The chapel hosts meaningful events to celebrate the importance of God in our lives. These events include Easter, Pentecost and Thanksgiving. In these events the music area participates with the praises

Gratitude

At Thanksgiving celebrations, students express their gratitude not only materially, but also spiritually, recognizing the blessings in their lives.

Spiritual formation

Through these activities and celebrations, we seek to instill in students a solid belief and faith towards God, promoting comprehensive spiritual development.
