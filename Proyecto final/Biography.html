Biography

Professor Pablo Andres Piza Velandia has been helping at the institution since 2012 when he began to provide accompaniment in the liturgical part, but later in 2015 he made the decision to leave. He returned later in 2017 but he joined the teachers and began teaching in the areas of music and religion.

A little of his love for music

The professor has always had a great admiration for music, a love that dates back to his childhood. However, it was at the age of 14 that this interest transformed into an active passion. It all started in a church, a place that played a fundamental role in both his spiritual and musical development. Professor Pablo was part of a Catholic group in the church, which was in charge of the choir.

Lúdica

On Tuesdays from 3:30pm to 5:30pm there are games which take place in the coliseum, in which they teach multiple instruments such as the piano, guitar and drums. Of these, Professor Pablo teaches piano and guitar while Professor Christian Alfonso Rios teaches drums.

Creating your own contest

The professor, during his time at the Seminario, created one of the most important events in the history of the school, which is TALOHA, which seeks to show the potential that people have, whether in areas musical, artistic or in the kitchen, with this what is sought is that the participants can demonstrate their talents to others and that others can see the talents that exist in the institution.
